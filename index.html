About Us

SewMates is a place for crafty individuals to come together and share the joys of craft-making! We have weekly events where you can come and join others who share your interests. For more info on upcoming events or to see where your closest meetings are take a look at out Crafting Calendar and Locations page.

We hope to see you soon!

Interests

SewMates caters to a wide spectrum of crafting interests:

  • Sewing
  • Scrapbooking
  • Knitting
  • Beading
  • Painting
  • Scultping
  • And So Much More!!!
